## Getting Started

### For Farmers üöú

#### 1. Create Your Account
1. Click **Sign Up** in the header
2. Choose a username (letters, numbers, and underscores only)
3. Create a secure password (minimum 6 characters)
4. Select **"Farmer - Sell my products"**
5. Enter your farm name and description
6. Click **Create Account**

#### 2. Add Your First Product
1. Navigate to **My Products** from the header
2. Click **Add Product**
3. Fill in the product details:
   - Product name (e.g., "Fresh Tomatoes")
   - Description (optional)
   - Category (Vegetables or Fruits)
   - Price per unit
   - Available quantity
   - Unit of measurement (default: kg)
4. Upload product images:
   - Click the upload area
   - Select one or multiple images
   - Images will be automatically compressed to under 1MB
5. Click **Create Product**

#### 3. Manage Your Products
- **Edit**: Click the Edit button on any product card
- **Delete**: Click the trash icon to remove a product
- **Update Inventory**: Edit the quantity as stock changes

#### 4. Process Orders
1. Go to **Orders** from the header
2. View all incoming orders with customer details
3. Update order status as you progress:
   - **Pending**: New order received
   - **Confirmed**: You've accepted the order
   - **Preparing**: Getting the order ready
   - **Ready for Pickup**: Order is packed
   - **Out for Delivery**: Order is on the way
   - **Delivered**: Order completed
4. View customer contact information and delivery address

---

### For Customers üõí

#### 1. Create Your Account
1. Click **Sign Up** in the header
2. Choose a username
3. Create a secure password
4. Select **"Customer - Buy fresh produce"**
5. Click **Create Account**

#### 2. Browse Products
1. Visit the **Home** page
2. Use the search bar to find specific products
3. Filter by category (Vegetables or Fruits)
4. Click **View Details** to see more information about a product

#### 3. Add to Cart
1. Click the shopping cart icon on any product
2. Or click **View Details** and add from the product page
3. View your cart by clicking **Cart** in the header

#### 4. Manage Your Cart
- **Increase Quantity**: Click the + button
- **Decrease Quantity**: Click the - button
- **Remove Item**: Click the trash icon
- View your total at any time

#### 5. Place an Order
1. Click **Proceed to Checkout** in your cart
2. Enter your delivery address
3. Provide your phone number
4. Add any special instructions (optional)
5. Review your order total
6. Click **Place Order**

#### 6. Track Your Orders
1. Go to **My Orders** from the header
2. View all your orders and their current status
3. See order details including:
   - Items ordered
   - Total amount
   - Delivery address
   - Order status

## Profile Management

### Update Your Profile
1. Click your username in the header
2. Select **Profile**
3. Update your information:
   - **All Users**: Phone number
   - **Farmers Only**: Farm name, location, and description
4. Click **Save Changes**

## Image Upload Guidelines

### Supported Formats
- JPEG (.jpg, .jpeg)
- PNG (.png)
- GIF (.gif)
- WEBP (.webp)
- AVIF (.avif)

### Requirements
- Maximum file size: 1MB (automatic compression applied)
- Filename: English letters and numbers only
- Recommended: Clear, well-lit photos of products

### Automatic Processing
- Images are automatically compressed if over 1MB
- Converted to WEBP format for optimal performance
- Resized to maximum 1920px resolution
- Quality optimized for fast loading

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Currently, password reset is not available. Please create a new account if needed.

**Q: Can I change my role from customer to farmer?**
A: No, roles are set during signup and cannot be changed.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: The website is fully responsive and works great on mobile browsers.

### For Farmers

**Q: How many products can I list?**
A: There's no limit! List as many products as you want.

**Q: Can I upload multiple images per product?**
A: Yes, you can upload multiple images for each product.

**Q: What happens if I run out of stock?**
A: Edit your product and set the quantity to 0, or delete the product listing.

### For Customers

**Q: Can I order from multiple farmers at once?**
A: Yes! Your cart can contain products from different farmers. Orders will be automatically grouped by farmer.

**Q: How do I cancel an order?**
A: Contact the farmer directly using the phone number provided in your order details.

**Q: Can I modify my order after placing it?**
A: No, but you can contact the farmer directly to request changes.
